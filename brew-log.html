Brew #001

Coffee: Café Santo Domingo Espresso

Roast: Dark Roast

Grind: Pre-ground espresso

Method: Bialetti 3-Cup Moka Pot

Dose: 13g

Output: 128g brewed coffee

Filter: E&B Lab by IMS Competition Moka Filter (3-Cup)

Filter Upgrade: Replaced original Bialetti metal filter with E&B Lab by IMS Competition Filter.

Paper Filter: Yes

Water Method: Warm water start

Notes: Cleaner taste and smoother cup. This brew used both the E&B Lab by IMS Competition Filter and a paper filter, which may have reduced oils and fine sediment.

Brew #002

Coffee: Café Santo Domingo Espresso

Roast: Dark Roast

Grind: Pre-ground espresso

Method: Bialetti 3-Cup Moka Pot

Dose: 13g

Filter: E&B Lab Competition Filter

Paper Filter: No

Output: 128g

Observations

  • Slightly more bitter than Brew #001
  • More coffee oils present
  • Fuller body
  • Less clean finish compared to paper filter brew

Next Experiment

Replace the E&B Lab Competition Filter with the original Bialetti filter and compare brews using both a paper filter and no paper filter. Results will be evaluated for bitterness, body, clarity, and sediment.

Equipment

  • Bialetti 3-Cup Moka Pot
  • E&B Lab by IMS Competition Moka Filter (3-Cup)
  • Digital Scale
  • Café Santo Domingo Espresso (Dark Roast)
  • AeroPress paper filter

Filter Comparison

  • Original Bialetti Filter: ~0.8 mm (800 µm)
  • E&B Lab IMS Filter: ~0.2 mm (200 µm)
  • Difference: Approximately 4× smaller openings

The smaller perforations may reduce the amount of fine coffee particles reaching the cup, potentially producing a cleaner brew.
